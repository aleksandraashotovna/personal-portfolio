About

I’m a Lead UX/UI Designer and Team Lead, who combines hands-on design with mentoring and process management. For the last decade, I've mainly been working on complex systems, but I also enjoy tackling smaller projects that allow for more creativity and experimentation. I also taught a UX/UI design course at IT Academy.

My biggest passion is building scalable processes that allow teams, small or big, to develop complex products efficiently, consistently and without reliance on a single person. Among my professional interests are design systems, accessibility and web development.
